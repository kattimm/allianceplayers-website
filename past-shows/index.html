Past Shows

An Evening With Edgar Allan Poe

Performed February 2017. Adapted by Robert Mason.

Romantic dinner and a unique evening out with the master of macabre... It's Poe like you've never seen before! Perfect for a chilly winter night of suspense and dread, and just in time for Valentines Day.

Guests enjoyed dinner, dessert, a glass of wine, and an unforgettable theatrical experience that left guests unsettled long after they left the venue.

The Brothers Grimm Spectaculathon

Performed November 2016. Written by Don Zolidis.

How do you fit all 209 Brothers Grimm fairy tales into one performance? With creativity, determination, and a little insanity!

Join our narrators as they string together the stories you think you know into an evening of creative license, sassy actors, audience participation, and just enough subversive silliness to keep mom and dad laughing too.

A Christmas Carol

Performed December 2015.

The classic Christmas story of a miserly old man and the spiritual visitors who teach him that there's more to life than material goods.
