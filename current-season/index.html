2017 - 2018 Season

'Night, Mother

by Marsha Norman

It's a night at home like any other for "Mama" and her adult daughter, Jessie... Until the moment when everything changes, and suddenly the two find themselves locked in a life or death battle of wills.

Skeletons are ripped out of the family closet in this Pulitzer Prize winning drama that forces you to take a closer look at what you think you know about the ones you love.

6 - 8 September

She Kills Monsters

by Qui Nguyen

A comedic romp into the world of fantasy role-playing games, She Kills Monsters tells the story of Agnes Evans as she leaves her childhood home in Ohio following the death of her teenage sister, Tilly. When Agnes finds Tilly's Dungeons & Dragons notebook, however, she stumbles into a journey of discovery and action-packed adventure in the imaginary world that was Tilly's refuge.

In this high-octane dramatic comedy laden with homicidal fairies, nasty ogres, and '90s pop culture, acclaimed playwright Qui Nguyen offers a heart-pounding homage to the geek and warrior within us all.

6 October, 12 - 14 October

An Evening of Shel Silverstein

by Shel Silverstein

Everyone remembers Where the Sidewalk Ends and A Light in the Attic, but few people realize that Shel Silverstein also wrote a play for adults!

Meet a side of Silverstein you never expected in "An Adult Evening of Shel Silverstein", while kids enjoy dramatic readings of the classic poems and stories you know and love. This event takes place in different rooms of the ICC, making it a fantastic night of fun for the entire family.

2 - 4 February

The Pillowman

by Martin McDonagh

This is the harrowing tale of Katurian, a fiction writer in an unnamed police state who is interrogated about the gruesome content of his short stories and their similarities to a number of bizarre child-murders occurring in his town. Imaginative and darkly twisted, The Pillowman is an over-the-top, jarring experience that grabs you, challenges you, and refuses to leave your head for days after leaving the theater.

14 - 17 March
