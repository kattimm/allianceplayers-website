"SHE KILLS MONSTERS" POSTPONED UNTIL SPRING 2018

After careful consideration, we have decided to delay the opening of “She Kills Monsters” until the spring. We apologize to everyone who has been looking forward to our performances next weekend, but we promise the delay will be worth it. The intense stage combat, amazing spectacle, and heartfelt story are sure to enthrall even the most discerning viewer, but we need more time to properly deliver a finished product. It is also very important that our young actors and actresses have enough time for their schoolwork, as many have demanding course loads. This spring, we will be treating you to a truly epic adventure like you’ve never seen on the Alliance Theater stage. Do not miss it.

This Friday, we will still be having a Sneak Peek of what is to come in partnership with NerdCon. Doors will open at 19:00 at the ICC on Friday, October 06. Entrance is only granted with a valid 2017 NerdCon ticket.

She Kills Monsters

by Qui Nguyen

A comedic romp into the world of fantasy role-playing games, She Kills Monsters tells the story of Agnes Evans as she leaves her childhood home in Ohio following the death of her teenage sister, Tilly. When Agnes finds Tilly's Dungeons & Dragons notebook, however, she stumbles into a journey of discovery and action-packed adventure in the imaginary world that was Tilly's refuge.

In this high-octane dramatic comedy laden with homicidal fairies, nasty ogres, and '90s pop culture, acclaimed playwright Qui Nguyen offers a heart-pounding homage to the geek and warrior within us all.
