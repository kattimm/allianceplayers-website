Our Mission:

To provide an outlet for participation in the performing arts for the JFC Brunssum and Tri-Border Community resulting in quality, affordable theatrical entertainment.

News

We're thrilled to announce that the Alliance Players were recognized for achievements in three categories at this year's TOPPERs awards!

Our 2017 TOPPERs Awards:

  • Judges' Choice Award: Creative Use of the Performance Space
  • Best Military Actor in a Play: Christopher Whyte
  • Best Stage Properties and Dressing for a Play

And just look at all these nominations!
  • Best Sound Design for a Play: Vincent Pinaula
  • Best Stage Manager for a Play: Harleigh Pinaula
  • Best Technical Director for a Play
  • Best Military Actor in a Play: Scott Eidson
  • Best Youth Performace in a Play: Coleton Stanley
  • Best Actor in a Featured Role in a Play: Harvey Toolan

Congratulations to the entire cast and crew of An Evening with Edgar Allan Poe for their hard work and dedication in bringing this show to the stage!
